Switching Languages with SynthMark XP.

 

See Also: Switching Themes, Back to Main.

 

For the convenience of those who do not speak English, we have made SynthMark XP’s new interface fully multi-lingual and can switch languages seamlessly. Like the themes switching menu, the languages menu is just beneath it and will allow you to change languages with the click of a button, even in the middle of a chat session.

 

Though installing a new language will only result in SynthMark XP finding this language once SynthMark XP has been restarted.

 

How to switch languages:

 

1.       Once SynthMark XP is open.

2.       Click on the view menu.

3.       Followed by the language sub-menu.

4.       You will now see a list of all the languages available for SynthMark XP.

5.       Simply click the one you want and the text will switch almost instantly.

 

 

Remaining Elements:

Please note that while switching languages, some elements may still remain in the old language selected, these are all the content related media, for example the previous chat text or some scoring words.
