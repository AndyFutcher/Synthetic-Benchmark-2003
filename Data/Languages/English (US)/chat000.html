Chatting, SynthMark XP provides you with the ability to chat with anyone using SynthMark XP in the world, besides all the benchmarking technology, communication is one of the things you will be doing most with other benchmarkers out there. The sheer volume of people who are going to have access to SynthMark XP out there means you may get some people who like cause fights, so dealing with these people is more than possible through commands.

 

After all, this is a competitive software program, so commands like kicking and banning come into their own. You can also whisper to a specific platform where no one else will see what you have said. Remember in this system, the host computer has all the power to kick and ban at will, whereas the clients can kick and ban but a vote it instantly called in that case.
