SynthMark™ XP Help Guide

 

This is the official help system for AndyFutcher SynthMark™ XP, that is if the quick help provided within SynthMark XP fail to explain things properly, then we can provide you with additional information both online and offline.

 

If you are not sure where the ‘quick help’ options are within SynthMark XP then simply look in the upper right hand corner of your SynthMark XP system and you will see a check box called Quick Help, place a check in its option box and you move your mouse over any of the main options to find out a little more than the obvious.
