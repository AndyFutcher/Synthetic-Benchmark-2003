The Information We Collect: We gather two types of information about users:

 

1. Non-personally identifiable information: When visitors come to our Site, we collect and aggregate information indicating, among other things, which pages were visited, the order in which they were visited, and which hyperlinks were "clicked." Collecting such information involves the logging of the IP addresses, operating system and browser software used by each visitor to our Site. Although such information is not personally identifiable, we can determine from the IP address a visitor's Internet Service Provider and the geographic location of his or her point of connectivity.

The non-personally identifiable information we collect helps us identify the most popular areas of our Site and determine the effectiveness of our promotional activities. In addition, we may disclose some of this aggregated, non-personally identifiable information to our advertisers to inform them of the number of people who have seen and/or clicked through their advertisements. For similar purposes, we use "cookies" - that is, small text files placed on your computer's hard drive - to help us determine the type of content and sites to which you link, the length of time you spend at any particular area of our site, and which of our services you choose to use. Cookies also tell us which advertising you have seen on our site. Essentially, cookies are your identification cards for our computers, or servers that delivered the advertising to you. Cookies are only read by the server that placed them, and are unable to execute any code or virus.

Cookies allow us to serve you better and more efficiently, and to personalize your experience at our Site. Nevertheless, you should be able to control how and whether cookies will be accepted by your web browser. For more information on how to do so, please refer to the documentation accompanying your browser. Please note that some of our third party advertisers and advertising agencies may place cookies on your computer's hard drive, in order to provide you with banner and other types of advertising that may be of interest to you while you are visiting our site. We do not have access to these cookies or any information that they may contain, and cannot and do not assume any responsibility for the actions or information collection practices of such third party advertisers or advertising agencies. Please contact the advertiser for information on these cookies as well as their information collection practices.

Personally identifiable information: In certain areas of the our Site - for example, when purchasing a product on-line, or registering for our mailing list - we ask that you provide us with personally identifiable information (including, for example, first name, surname, street address, email address, telephone number, and when applicable, credit card information).

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children (defined in this Privacy Policy as minors younger than 13 years of age) without obtaining parental consent. Before and during the registration process, we encourage children to include a parent or guardian in the process.

We do not control the acts of our visitors. All visitors should be aware that, when they disclose personally identifiable information in a public area of our Site, the information may be collected and used by others for the purposes of distributing unsolicited communications to you or to others outside our Site. If you encounter any user of our Site who is improperly collecting or using information about you or other users, please contact us.
