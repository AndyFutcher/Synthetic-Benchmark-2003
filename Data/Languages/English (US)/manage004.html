Using the Send To Feature.

 

See Also: Sending your Projects to us, Back to Main.

 

The ‘Send To’ feature is available to full version users only, and allows you to export your projects quickly to many different folders on your computer or email addresses of friends. It also has the Send To Andy Futcher feature where we will review you projects and you could win an award for certain projects with any of the 24 main projects. See the next chapter for more information.

 

You can find the ‘Send To’ option under the ‘File’ drop down menu.

 

 

The different Send To targets:

 

·          Send to the desktop saves a copy of the project in exported form on your computers desktop.

 

·          Send to a mail recipient automatically creates a new email for you can attaches a copy of the current project in exported form into the email. Great to quickly sending a copy of the results of the current benchmarks.

 

·          Send to my documents is exactly like the send to the desktop option but places a exported project in your ‘my documents’ folder.

 

·          Finally and most importantly, if you want to compete for the best of the best benchmarks in the world, send you projects to us and we may even publish your projects on our hall of fame section on our site.
