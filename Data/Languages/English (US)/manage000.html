Managing your benchmarks with SynthMark XP

 

See Also: Actually Benchmarking, Using NETMark, The Content Manager, Back to Main.

 

Part of making your benchmarks most useful, is managing them correctly. Some options help you save your project exactly as you left it, others help you export them to rich text files for easy editing and exporting them to whatever you need. Other features allow you to send us your projects for publishing on our site, this includes the topbox award. SynthMark XP also has a great printing system for customized print-outs from any section of your project.

 

As if that was not enough SynthMark XP can also help you email your friends your scores, weather they have SynthMark XP or not. The default SynthMark XP file format includes powerful compression and error checking, as well as backward compatibility with all older versions of SynthMark XP.

Saving for the Web*

Got that urge to publish your projects online? This tool is enabled for full version users only.

The Revert feature

Similar to the undo feature, but reloads your project as it exists on your hard drive.

The ‘Send To’ menu*

Do you hate having to manage files, so we made it easy for you to send your projects to any common place.

Sending your Projects to us*

Use this feature if you want to for the topbox awards or simply get it published online for others to see.

Printing your Projects

Send your projects to any printer or fax driver to get a hard copy of your project.

You will find all of these options under the ‘file’ drop down menu and some of them on the tool bar too.
