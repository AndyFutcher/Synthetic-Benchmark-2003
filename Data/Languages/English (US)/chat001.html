Chatting in Public Mode with SynthMark XP.

 

See Also: Whispering to a Specific Person, Back to Main.

 

Remember that SynthMark XP has two different types of chatting modes; the first mode allows you to say something to everyone on the server, and this is the mode you will most likely use more often.

 

While you are in chat mode there is a list of all the connected people in this server, the list is spilt into two columns, the first column shows you the users name and the second one tells you if they are using the full version or the trial version. This is important because you cannot compete with someone in a Hard drive benchmark if you have the full version and they don’t, unlucky for them. Although they can tell you what they go offline, they have to come online and prove it ;-).

 

Just above the connected users like there is a drop down list that has (initially) two main titles, the first one says ‘Normal Public Chat’ and while this box says that, whatever you say will be said to everyone on the server.
